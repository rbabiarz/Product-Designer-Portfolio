# Parlay Games — iGaming at Production Volume

**User:** hiring manager / design leader gauging range and production discipline; the case proves
the "one designer across a whole platform surface" claim behind the connected-lighting → defense
narrative (the career chapter *before* IoT).

**Thesis:** breadth under real constraints. One designer shipping game art, brand systems, player
sites, promotions, and operator tooling for a live real-money platform — where every asset had to
perform on minimum-spec machines, in nine markets, on whatever runtime the era offered
(Java → Flash → HTML → mobile).

**What the pages prove:** the work is real and at volume — logo systems with palettes and type
specimens (Pow Wow Bingo, Titanium Gaming, Keto Konnection, Radio Bingo, Chatter Box, Sports Plays
Poker), a 40-avatar caricature set, bingo/keno/slots game screens (75/80/90-ball buy flows,
Hollywood Reels, 7s Ablaze, BonBon Blast, Bushwacks, Word Dragon), the Wonder Bingo social
platform, Radio Bingo banking flows, the parlaygames.com corporate site, seasonal promo banners at
three fixed production sizes with localization, back-office financial/management tooling, and
Lightwave 3D casino imagery.

**Scope:** two portfolio pages (quick tour `parlay-gaming-showcase.html`, deep dive
`parlay-gaming.html`), preview tile, full site registration (switchers, homepages, work page,
concierge, search index, sitemap). Case study #09.

**States:** image-led showcase pages — loading (lazy below fold), missing-image alt text, reduced
motion; no live embeds in v1 of these pages.

**Source:** the prior Figma case study (Claude-Design-Portfolio, node 31-2698) — copy, stats, and
all imagery extracted from it. Headline figures ($2B USD wagered per year, 2.8M players online,
70 clients, 50+ games, world leader in online bingo across UK/Spain/Italy/Kenya/Denmark/US/Brazil/
Bahamas/Canada) are Parlay-era company figures from that case study, labeled as such on-page —
they are platform outcomes the design work served, not metrics claimed for a single design.
