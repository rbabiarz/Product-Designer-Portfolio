Senior Product Designer and Experience Design Leader. BFA with Honours, York University. Experience across enterprise SaaS / B2B, enterprise IoT, regulated iGaming, mobile, AR, and connected systems. Builder by instinct.

I design interfaces for systems where operator error carries real-world cost: networked cyber-physical IoT, IEC 62443-certified industrial platforms, enterprise B2B SaaS, and real-time regulated transaction systems. I ship novel interaction models fast, co-develop with operators in the field, and have earned design a founding voice inside engineering-first organizations. The primitives I work in — anomaly dashboards, confirmation-gated destructive actions, role-scoped access, provable audit trails — carry across any domain where decisions have consequences. I've worked in both fast-moving startup environments where decisions happen quickly and large corporate structures with formal process.
