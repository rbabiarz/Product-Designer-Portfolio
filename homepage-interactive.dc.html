// AEGIS · Watch Floor · Classification Drill

Read the trace,
not the number.

AEGIS is a defense decision-intelligence concept, a watch floor that fuses dozens of sensor and intelligence feeds into one picture an operator can act on, fast. Its thesis: the AI brings receipts, not verdicts. It hands you fused tracks with their sources, a confidence score, and the evidence it flagged against itself, then it stops. It won't make the call. You will. This is that idea, made playable, classify each contact before it breaches the inner ring.
